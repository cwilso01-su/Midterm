Bridal Veil Falls

Felicia Day

This was a refreshing hike through well managed trail to one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the state! The observation deck was a great place for a picnic and a perfect opportunity for gorgeous photos of the falls. A must for any outdoor adventurer and a photographer’s dream!

Cozy Ghost Town Coffee

Dave Frankfort

If you want to chill, this is one of my favorite places. I met the most amazing people there. It is a relaxed spot and a nice place to relax before or after all the craziness that is skiing.

The Nightlife!

Serena Wilbon

A fun, relaxing place to discover some great bands. There are so many bars and restaurants filled with live music and good atmosphere. Definitely worth the trip!
